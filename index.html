ABOUT US

SSWL relies on a set-up of channel

its competitors.

internet stems from its consistent support to
the endeavour to digitise India and make
internet accessibility easier for all.

team of 5 members and is growing with considerable pace.

speed and efficient internet services to all regions of the city.
It caters to an ever-increasing domestic and commercial
customer service base. Its competitive prices and efficient
team makes the company a reliable and sustainable choice.
